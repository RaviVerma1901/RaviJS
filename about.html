Who We Are Meet The Founders

Dr. Ravi Prabhat
MD, Consultant Pathology

Dr. Ravi Prabhat:- has done his MBBS from the prestigious GRANT MEDICAL COLLEGE AND SIR J.J. HOSPITAL, MUMBAI and MD- Pathology from MUHS. After that he has working experience at G. B. Pant hospital, Institute of liver and biliary sciences (ILBS) and Maharshi Valmiki hospital, New Delhi. Currently he is posted as SMO in the department of Pathology at Sri Krishna Medical College and Hospital, Muzaffarpur. He has experience of 5 years in diagnostic pathology. His special interest is in Cytopathology, Histopathology And Hematology. He has published 6 papers in both national and international indexed journals.

Dr. Poonam Kumari
MBBS, DNB (Obstetrics and Gynecology)

Dr. Poonam Kumari:- completed MBBS from Agartala Government Medical College, Agartala and DNB (Obstetrics and Gynecology) from Baba Saheb Ambedkar College and Hospital, Rohini, New Delhi. She has completed her senior residency in Infertility and Reproductive Science from Maharshi Valmiki Hospital. New Delhi. She has been an outstanding student throughout her study period. She is an expert in Infertility and Reproductive medicine. Her field of interest also includes gynecologic oncology, obstetrics, gynecology, uterine cancer, cervical cancer, ovarian cancer, and gynecologic surgery and preservation for women diagnosed with cancer.
